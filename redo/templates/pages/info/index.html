cherrypicker

Cherrypicker was created by Andrew McGill. He works for The Atlantic.

This app is powered by data from the District of Columbia's Urban Forestry Administration. Only "street trees" are included in this app—no trees on private property or in federal parks. So don't be surprised if you see a tree in front of you that's not showing up here :-)

Thanks to DDOT Deputy Associate Director Earl Eutsler for his help on this project.
